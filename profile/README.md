A powerful, open-style **StudioOne 5** digital audio workstation designed for modern music production. ## About StudioOne 5
**StudioOne 5** is a professional-grade audio production program built specifically for songwriters, producers, and audio engineers.  
It provides access to advanced audio and MIDI features (multi-track recording, real-time time stretching, pitch correction, and console emulation), enabling deep sound design, live performance tracking, DDP export, and full project management.  
**StudioOne 5** offers studio-level functionality while remaining intuitive and efficient, making it an essential tool for both home studio enthusiasts and professional facilities.  
**StudioOne 5** supports VST, AU, and ReWire plugins, and includes a rich library of virtual instruments, loops, and samples. With **StudioOne 5**, you can produce anything from podcasts to platinum records.  
**StudioOne 5** also features a unique drag-and-drop workflow, chord detection, harmonic editing, and arranger track for rapid song prototyping. Whether you compose orchestral scores or electronic beats, **StudioOne 5** adapts to your style.
